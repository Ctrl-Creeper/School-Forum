Welcome to the A-Level Courses Discussion Forum! This is a space for students to share experiences about A-Level courses. Discuss teacher grading styles, course difficulty, workload, or tips for success. Rules: Keep it respectful—no personal attacks on teachers or students. Posts are moderated.

1. Mathematics (数学) ▼
MathGuru2025 (Year 13)
A-Level Maths is intense, especially Mechanics and Pure. Ms. Thompson grades fairly but marks harshly on careless errors. Difficulty: 7/10. Tip: Master past papers for A*.
NumberNinja (Year 12)
Pure Maths is okay, but Statistics trips me up. Mr. Lee gives good feedback, but exams are tough. Workload: ~8 hours/week.
2. Further Mathematics (进阶数学) ▼
FurtherFan (Year 13)
Further Maths is NOT for the faint-hearted. Complex numbers and matrices are brutal. Dr. Patel is strict but fair; expect A if you nail proofs. Workload: 10+ hours/week.
3. Chinese (语文) ▼
LitStarCN (Year 13)
Chinese is heavy on essay writing and classical texts. Ms. Li grades based on depth of analysis; non-natives struggle. Difficulty: 6/10 for natives, 8/10 for others.
4. Physics (物理) ▼
Physicist2025 (Year 13)
Physics is challenging—waves and electricity are tough. Mr. Brown’s practicals are fun, but his tests are hard. Aim for A* with consistent revision. Workload: 7-8 hours/week.
