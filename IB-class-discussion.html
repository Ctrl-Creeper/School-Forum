1. Math Analysis and Approaches (AA) HL/SL (数学AA HL/SL) ▼
MathWhiz2024 (Senior)
AA HL is tough if you're not a math person—lots of proofs and calculus. SL is more manageable for those wanting basics. Mr. Johnson grades fairly but expects detailed workings; average scores around 5-6. Tips: Practice past papers religiously!
CalcStruggler (Junior)
Agreed on HL difficulty—it's like uni level. But Ms. Lee's SL class is chill; she gives partial credit generously.
Anonymous
HL workload is insane, 10+ hours/week. Predicted 7 if you grind.
2. Math Applications and Interpretation (AI) HL/SL (数学AI HL/SL) ▼
StatsFan (Senior)
AI focuses on real-world apps like stats and modeling. HL is challenging with tech tools, but SL is straightforward. Dr. Patel grades based on IA quality—easy 6s if you use Desmos well.
AppInterpreter (Junior)
SL is perfect for non-STEM majors. Difficulty: 4/10. Ms. Kim is strict on exams but helpful in class.
HLHustler
HL ramps up with advanced stats; expect 7-8 hours/week. Grading is consistent, no curves.
3. Chinese A HL/SL (语文A HL/SL) ▼
LitLoverCN (Senior)
HL dives deep into classical texts and essays—high difficulty for non-natives. SL is more balanced. Mr. Wang grades holistically; loves creative interpretations, averages 5-6.
